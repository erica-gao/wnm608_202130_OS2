LATTE

How to make a latte without an espresso machine

INGREDIENTS
  • 2 tablespoon espresso powder (or 2 tablespoons instant coffee granules/powder if you don't have a coffee machine)
  • 1 cup hot water
  • Any kind of milk you like: whole, 2%, oatmilk, soymilk etc.
  • Ice (Optional)
  1. Grab a mug of your choice.
  2. Pour in a milk of your choice, here I'm using coconut milk :)
  3. Prepare your coffee machine for brewing, or boil some water if you are using instant coffee powder.
  4. Add 1-2 tablespoons of coffee powder and start the machine! Be aware that since we are using coffee machine to brew espresso, add only 1 cup of water.
  5. It's ready!
  6. Pour in and serve!

FRENCH TOAST

With Pumpkin Toast

INGREDIENTS
  • 1-2 egg(s)
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of cinnamon (Optional)
  • Butter (or vegetable oil)
  • Bread slices
  1. Prepare some bread slices. I'm using pumpkin toast!
  2. Crack 1-2 eggs to a plate. Since I'm only cooking 1 toast, I only used 1 egg.
  3. Add 3/4 cup of milk in it and mix it up with the egg.
  4. Spread vegetable oil (or butter) on a wok and heat it up with mid-high heat.
  5. Dip the toast into egg + milk mix and soak it up on both sides.
  6. Toss the toast into the wok and turn the heat down to low-medium.
  7. Flip to the other side.
  8. Serve by adding a little bit of sugar on the top (Totally optional)

EGG

Sunny-side up

INGREDIENTS
  • 1-2 egg(s)
  • Butter (or vegetable oil)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Spread vegetable oil (or butter) on a wok and turn to medium heat.
  2. Crack 1 egg to the wok.
  3. When it's starting to cook, add a little bit of salt.
  4. And pepper.
  5. Put on the lid (very important) and turn to low heat.
  6. When there's egg white on top of the yolk it's getting ready. You can wait longer if you want.
  7. Ready to serve!
